Bulls and Cows

Enter a four-digit number with no duplicated digits. For example, "6329" is valid, while "6323" is not.

Click "Submit Number" to compare your number against the secret number. If your guess contains a digit that matches one of the digits in the secret number and is in the same position, that is considered one bull.

If your guess contains a digit that matches one of the digits in the secret number but is in a different position, that is considered one cow.
